We are Code for NL

Code for NL is the Dutch community of people working together on open, fair, and inclusive digital government and society.

Our goal is the successful digital transformation of local governments, central government, and society as a whole.

Code for NL is an independent spin-off of waag and governing partner of the international Code for All network.
